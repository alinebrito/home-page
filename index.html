Aline Brito
Ph.D. student at UFMG
Software developer

Aline Brito is a Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Department (DCC/Aserg) at the Federal University of Minas Gerais (UFMG), where she also received a Master's Degree in Computer Science. She received a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Engineering from the Pontifical Catholic University of Minas Gerais (PUC Minas). Brito also was a software developer at Concert Technologies for five years.
