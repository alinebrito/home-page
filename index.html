Aline Brito

I received my PhD in Computer Science (2023) from the Universidade Federal de Minas Gerais (Aserg Group/UFMG), where I also received a Master's Degree in Computer Science (2018). Currently, I am a Professor in the Department of Software Engineering at Pontifícia Universidade Católica de Minas Gerais (PUC Minas), where I also received a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Engineering (2014). I was also a software developer for five years and a temporary Professor for about two years at Department of Computer Science from Centro Federal de Educação Tecnológica de Minas Gerais (CEFET-MG).
Research interests: Software evolution, software testing, software repository mining, software quality, and empirical software engineering.
